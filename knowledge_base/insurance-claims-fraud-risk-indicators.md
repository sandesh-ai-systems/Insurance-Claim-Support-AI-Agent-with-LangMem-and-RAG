# Claims Fraud Risk Indicators (Screening Reference)

## Common Risk Signals

- Policy recently incepted before loss event
- Incident reported long after event date without valid reason
- Repeated claims for similar damage pattern
- Inconsistent incident descriptions across statements
- Repair estimate significantly above benchmark ranges
- Minimal or no scene evidence when severe damage is claimed

## Verification Actions

- Cross-check incident timeline with call logs and metadata
- Compare repair estimate against approved network benchmarks
- Request additional photos/videos with geotag/time metadata when available
- Verify police report authenticity and case number mapping

## Escalation Rules

Escalate to SIU/manual fraud review when:

- multiple high-risk indicators appear together
- forged/altered document suspicion exists
- claimant/provider has unusual repeated pattern clusters

## Communication Policy

Do not accuse claimant of fraud in customer-facing communication.
Use neutral phrasing: "additional verification required".
