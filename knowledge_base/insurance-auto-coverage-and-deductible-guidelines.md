# Auto Coverage and Deductible Guidelines

## Common Coverage Types

### Collision
Covers damage to insured vehicle from collision with another vehicle or object, subject to deductible.

### Comprehensive
Covers non-collision losses such as theft, vandalism, fire, flood, hail, and falling objects, subject to deductible.

### Liability (Property Damage / Bodily Injury)
Covers damages/injuries caused to third parties when insured is at fault. Does not cover insured vehicle damage.

### Uninsured / Underinsured Motorist
May cover insured losses when at-fault party has no or insufficient insurance, based on policy endorsements.

## Deductible Handling

- Deductible applies per covered loss event.
- Payout calculation = approved covered loss amount - deductible.
- If approved covered loss amount is less than deductible, payable amount may be zero.

## Coverage Positioning Rules

- Preliminary recommendation must reference policy section and endorsement context.
- If facts are incomplete, recommend "pending documents" rather than denial.
- Final approve/deny decision must be marked as adjuster-reviewed.

## Communication Guardrail

Do not state final denial language without explicit adjuster confirmation.
