# Auto Claim Required Documents by Claim Type

## Collision Claims

- Driver's license copy
- Vehicle registration copy
- Scene photos (all sides and damage close-ups)
- Repair estimate from approved shop or certified estimate source
- Police report (if available)

## Comprehensive (Theft / Vandalism / Natural Peril)

- FIR/police complaint for theft or vandalism
- Proof of ownership and key inventory (for theft)
- Photos/videos of damage
- Incident date/time declaration

## Glass Damage

- Damage photo showing crack/chip spread
- Vehicle details and policy number
- Repair/replacement invoice (post-approval or as required)

## Bodily Injury Involvement

- Medical reports and treatment summary
- Hospital bills and discharge summary
- Police report / incident report
- Contact details of treating provider

## Document Quality Requirements

- Clear, readable scans/photos
- File timestamps preserved where available
- No redacted critical identifiers (policy number, report number)
