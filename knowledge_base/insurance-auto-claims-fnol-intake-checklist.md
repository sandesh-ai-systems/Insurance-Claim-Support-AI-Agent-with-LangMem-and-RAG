# Auto Claims FNOL Intake Checklist

## Required FNOL Fields

- Claimant full name
- Policy number
- Incident date and time
- Incident location (city, state, and road/highway if applicable)
- Vehicle details (make, model, year, VIN if available)
- Brief incident narrative
- Third-party involvement details
- Police report status and report number (if filed)
- Injury status and emergency response involvement

## Intake Validation Rules

- Policy number must be present and match policy system format.
- Incident date cannot be in the future.
- Narrative must include at least one impact detail (who/what was hit, lane/road condition, or point of impact).
- If bodily injury is reported, mark claim for urgent adjuster review.

## Immediate Escalation Triggers

- Suspected intoxication or hit-and-run
- Multiple injured parties
- Commercial vehicle involvement
- Conflicting statements between claimant and third-party
- Large-loss estimate above internal threshold

## Recommended First Response Template Points

- Acknowledge claim receipt and empathy statement
- Confirm FNOL reference/claim ID
- List next required documents
- Provide expected timeline for initial adjuster contact
- Clarify that coverage decision follows adjuster review
