# Claims Settlement SLA and Communication Standards

## Internal SLA Targets

- FNOL acknowledgement: within 2 business hours
- First adjuster review touchpoint: within 1 business day
- Document sufficiency check: within 1 business day of receipt
- Preliminary coverage recommendation: within 2 business days after required docs

## Communication Standards

- Use plain language and avoid legal jargon where possible.
- Clearly separate:
  - confirmed facts
  - pending verification points
  - next action items
- Provide explicit timeline expectations for each pending step.

## Pending Information Messaging

If documents are missing, response should include:

- exact missing document names
- why each is needed
- submission channels
- expected turnaround after submission

## Sensitive Outcome Messaging

- Potential denial language should be framed as "under review" until adjuster confirmation.
- Include review rights/escalation path where required by policy/regulation.
